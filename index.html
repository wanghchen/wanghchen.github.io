About Me

My name is Huanchen Wang. I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science, The City University of Hong Kong (CityU) from Sep. 2022 under CityU-SUSTech Joint PhD Programme, supervised by Prof. Zhicong Lu (CityU) and Prof. Yuxin Ma (SUSTech). I am a member of DEER Lab and DVA Lab.

I received my BEng degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in July, 2022.

My current research mainly focuses on the area of Visual Analytics and Social Computing .
